Create Build

Begin by clicking one of the buttons on the left-hand side of the page. You will be taken to a list of parts in that respective category. Browse the parts and make a selection from available parts. Once you've selected all the parts you'd like included in your build, using the Check Compatibility feature will prompt PC Builder to notify you of any incompatibilities present in the build. 

Not sure which part to pick? You can always save your build for a later time, or share your build to a friend.
